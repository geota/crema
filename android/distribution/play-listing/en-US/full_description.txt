Crema is an open-source companion for the Decent Espresso DE1.

Connect over Bluetooth to drive your espresso machine and a coffee scale, dial in shots with editable pressure/flow profiles, and keep a searchable history of every shot with live charts.

Features:
* Live brew control — pressure, flow, temperature and weight on a real-time chart
* Profile editor — drag the pressure/flow curve, edit phases and limits, import/export
* Bean & roaster library — track freshness, ratings and grind; import from Beanconqueror
* Shot history — review, rate, compare and export your shots
* Scale support — many BLE coffee scales, capability-driven (tare, timer, flow, settings)
* Visualizer.coffee sync — upload and share your shots
* Tablet and phone layouts
