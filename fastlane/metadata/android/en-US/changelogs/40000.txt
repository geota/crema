Welcome to Crema — a fast, open-source companion for the Decent Espresso DE1.

• Live brew dashboard: real-time pressure, flow, temperature & weight
• 88 built-in profiles, plus create and edit your own
• Shot history with bean tracking and overlay compare
• Bluetooth scales: Bookoo, Acaia, Decent & more
• Optional Visualizer sync and Google Drive backup

Local-first and free. Unofficial; not affiliated with Decent Espresso.
